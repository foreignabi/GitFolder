About Our Pizza's

This veggie pizza recipe will delight vegetarians and carnivores alike. It’s fresh and full of flavor, handmade with gluten free dough (optional) featuring: Green bell peppers, Olives, Gran burger and Red onion. You’ll find a base of rich tomato sauce and golden, bubbling mozzarella underneath, of course.







Our cheese pizza is handmade with fluffy gluten free dough. This cheese pizza is for persons who prefer a simple taste. You can choose between stuffed or thin crust to suite your preference and we offer a variety of cheeses to choose from such as Mozzarella, Cheddar, Provolone, and Pecorino-Romano.







This is not your typical pepperoni pizza our pepperoni pizza has a special touch it is a deep dish pizza with stuff cheese crust topped with the freshest Pepperoni, Mozzarella cheese, red chilli peppers, sweet chilli tomato sauce and garnished with fresh picked parsley.








For all you meat lovers we have a specialized pizza that will make your mouth water. Dressed in fresh meat toppings such as: Bacon, Ham, Pepperoni, and Hot sausage with the option of not one but two types of cheeses baked in to a thin garlic crust.










Hawaiian pizza is known for its Sweet chilli tomato sauce, Mozzarella cheese, pineapples and the choice between two juicy meats Ham or Bacon. This pizza will surely bring an explosion of flavors to your mouth.
